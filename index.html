So, what is CSS ? let me explain you first____

CSS, or Cascading Style Sheets , is a stylesheet language used to control the appearance of a website. It allows developers to style and layout web pages by adjusting colors, fonts, spacing, and overall design. By separating content (HTML) from presentation (CSS), it makes maintaining and updating the look of a website easier and more efficient. CSS also enables responsive design, ensuring websites look good on different devices, from desktops to smartphones.
